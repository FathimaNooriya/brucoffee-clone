ABOUT US

More than Coffee

Bru coffeehouse was created as a part of EveryONE Matters Ministries, a 501c3 non-profit serving those in housing crisis.

Our mission is to alleviate and prevent homelessness through stable housing and case management services.

Our Cafe

Our cafe is directly across from the Blue Goose Event Center right in the heart of Loomis, CA.

We have something delicious for everyone- coffee, espresso, smoothies, tea, hot chocolate and delicious goodies!

Grab a friend or two, work or study, or just come by for a grab and go treat!
